BAO Yang (鲍杨)

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Data and Business Intelligence at Antai College of Economics and Management, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). Prior to joining SJTU, I was a Research Fellow in the Division of Accounting at the Business School of Nanyang Technological University. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Information Systems from the School of Computing at the National University of Singapore, and my bachelor's and master's degrees from Nanjing University.
