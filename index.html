Hi, I'm BAO Yang (鲍杨). Download CV

I am currently an Associate Professor at the Antai College of Economics and Management (ACEM), Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), Shanghai, China. Before joining SJTU, I have worked as Research Fellow in the Division of Accounting, Nanyang Business School, Nanyang Technological University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Information Systems from the School of Computing at the National University of Singapore, and bachelor & master degree from Nanjing University.
